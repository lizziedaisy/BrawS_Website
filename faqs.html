FAQs

BrawStats was originally designed to teach students about research design and analysis, highlighting the importance of design decisions on research outcomes.

It has grown into software which can be used by beginners, learners, and researchers alike, for most statistical and research needs.

BrawStats is capable of taking any set of parameters and simulating unlimited data sets, or can be used to analyse and explore already existing data sets.

BrawStats is for everyone. It has an easy to use interface packed with behind the scenes help documents, and can be accessed at user levels ranging from Beginner to Expert. It is perfect for those new to research, dissertation students, and anyone wanting to tackle complex ideas or explore their own data.

BrawStats is also useful as a teaching tool, demonstrating important statistical concepts in an easy to understand graphical form. Educators can purchase support documents and learning packs to run classes or entire modules based around this style of statistical teaching.

BrawStats can also be used simply to make nice graphs!
